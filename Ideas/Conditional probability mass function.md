The **conditional probability mass function** takes a regular [[Probability mass function|PMF]], and conditions it on a [[Random event|random event]], or another [[Random variable|random variable]].

Conditioning on an [[Random event|event]]:  
$$p_{X|A}(x)=P(X=x|A)=\frac{P(\{X=x\}\cap A)}{P(A)}$$
Conditioning on a [[Random variable|random variable]]: 
$$p_{X|Y}(x|y)=\frac{P(X=x,Y=y)}{P(Y=y)}=p_{X,Y}(x,y)/p_Y(y)$$
