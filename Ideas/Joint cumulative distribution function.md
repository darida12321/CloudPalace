The joint [[Cumulative distribution function|cumulative distribution function]] of two [[Random variable|random variables]] is:
$$F_{X,Y}(x,y)=P(X\leq x,Y\leq y)$$



| [[Discrete random variable\|Discrete]]                           | [[Continuous random variable\|Continuous]]                            |
| :--------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| $$F_{X,Y}(x,y)=\sum_{s\leq x}\sum_{t\leq y}p_{X,Y}(s,t)$$        | $$F_{X,Y}(x,y)=\int_{-\infty}^x\int_{-\infty}^yf_{X,Y}(s,t)\;dt\;ds$$ |
| $$p_{X,Y}(x,y)=??????????$$(When $X$ only takes integer values.) | $$f_{X,Y}(x,y)=\frac{\partial^2 F_{X,Y}}{\partial x\partial y}(x,y)$$ |
