A [[Probability law|probability law]] must follow these 3 axioms, defined by **Kolmogorov**:
- **Nonnegativity**: $\forall A.P(A)\geq 0$
- **Additivity**: If $A$ and $B$ are [[Disjoint sets|disjoint]], then $P(A\cup B)=P(A)+P(B)$.
- **[[Normalization property|Normalization]]**: $P(\Omega)=1$ (used generally as the [[Normalization property|normalization property]])
