If a [[Disjoint sets|disjoint]] set of [[Random event|events]] $A_1\dots A_n$ forms a [[Partition of a set|partition]] of the [[Sample spaces|sample space]], we can calculate the probability of an [[Random event|event]] $B$ by:
$$
\begin{align}
P(B)&=P(A_1\cap B)+\dots+P(A_n\cap B))\\
&=P(A_1)P(B|A_1)+\dots+P(A_n)P(B|A_n)

\end{align}
$$
