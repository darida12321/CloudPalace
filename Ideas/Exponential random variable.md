A [[Continuous random variable|continuous random variable]], which describes the distance between events that occur independently, and at a constant rate. It is analogous to the [[Geometric random variable|geometric random variable]].
$$
f_X(x)=
\begin{cases}
\lambda e^{-\lambda x}&\quad\text{if }0\leq x, \\
0&\quad\text{otherwise},
\end{cases}
$$

| [[Expectation\|Mean]] | [[Variance\|Variance]] |
| --------------------- | ---------------------- |
| $$1/\lambda$$         | $$1/\lambda^2$$        |
