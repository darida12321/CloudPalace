For finite [[Sample spaces|sample spaces]], the **probability**of an [[Probabilistic events|event]] is the sum of the probabilities of its elements. $P(\{s_1,s_2,\dots,s_n\})=P(\{s_1\})+P(\{s_2\})+\dots+P(\{s_n\})$
