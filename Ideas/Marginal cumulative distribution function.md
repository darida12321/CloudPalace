From a [[Joint cumulative distribution function|joint CDF]], you can calculate the **marginal CDF** by:
$$F_X(x)=\lim_{y\rightarrow\infty}F_{X,Y}(x,y)$$
This is generalizable for higher dimensions:
$$F_{X,Y}(x,y)=\lim_{z\rightarrow\infty}F_{X,Y,Z}(x,y,z)$$
$$F_Y(y)=\lim_{x\rightarrow\infty}\lim_{z\rightarrow\infty}F_{X,Y,Z}(x,y,z)$$
