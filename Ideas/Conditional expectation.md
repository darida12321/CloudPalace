
The **conditional expectation** is an [[Expectation|expectation]] that can be conditioned on either a [[Random event|random event]], or a [[Random variable|random variable]].

Conditioned on an [[Random event|event]]:$$E[X|A]=\sum_xxp_{X|A}(x|A)$$Conditioned on a [[Discrete random variable|discrete random variable]]:
$$E[X|Y=y]=\sum_xxp_{X|Y}(x|y)$$
Conditioned on a [[Continuous random variable|continuous random variable]]:
$$E[X|A]=\int_{-\infty}^\infty xf_{X|A}(x)dx$$
